Designing for Creativity: UI/UX for Kids

How our simple design choices help kids focus on having fun.

Published 2025-08-22

When designing for children, the best interface is one they don't have to think about. Hereâ€™s how we designed Creative Coloring to be intuitive and distraction-free.

Large, Tappable Buttons

The color palette and save button are large and easy to tap, making them perfect for little hands on touch screens. This reduces frustration and helps children develop fine motor skills.

Simple, Clear Controls

We use a simple "paint bucket" mechanic: pick a color, then tap a shape. There are no complex tools or menus, so kids can focus entirely on their creative choices.

A Distraction-Free Canvas

Our game is free of ads, pop-ups, and unnecessary text. The clean, focused layout helps children concentrate on the act of painting, improving their attention span and engagement.
