5 Fun Painting Ideas for Toddlers

Simple activities to make digital painting even more engaging for young kids.

Published 2025-08-22

Our painting game is designed for simplicity, but you can make the experience even more enriching with these fun ideas.

1. Color by Name

Call out a color name and have your toddler find it in the palette. This turns painting into a fun game of color recognition.

2. Tell a Story

As they color the house, ask questions like, "Who lives here?" or "What color should the door be for our friends?" This encourages storytelling and imagination.

3. Pattern Play

Challenge them to create a pattern. For example, can they make the windows alternate between two colors? This introduces a simple and fun logical challenge.

4. Themed Coloring

Suggest a theme, like a "sunny day house" or a "spooky nighttime house," and see how they use colors to bring that theme to life.

5. Celebrate Their Art

When they finish, use the "Save" button and make a big deal of their creation! Displaying their art on a tablet or printing it out reinforces their sense of accomplishment.
