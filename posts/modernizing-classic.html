From Sketch to Screen: The Making of a Coloring Page

A behind-the-scenes look at how we design our pictures.

Published 2025-08-22

Ever wonder how our digital coloring pictures are made? It's a simple process focused on creating fun, easy-to-color images for kids.

1. The Idea

We start with simple, recognizable themes that kids love, like houses, animals, or vehicles. The goal is to create a friendly and inviting picture.

2. The Digital Sketch

Using vector software, we draw the image with clean, bold lines. We make sure each area to be colored is a distinct, closed shape. This is the key to making the "paint bucket" fill work perfectly.

3. Testing for Fun

Before adding it to the game, we test the picture to ensure all areas are easy to tap and fill. We check that the lines are thick enough and that there are no tiny, frustrating spots to color. The result is a fun, ready-to-paint picture for your child to enjoy!
